Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

MakersAI Studio is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: We store your design prompts and generated models locally on your device using SQLite.
  • API Usage: We track API call counts to manage rate limiting and prevent abuse.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate 3D models and laser cutting designs based on your prompts
  • To cache results and improve performance
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent API abuse
  • To improve the quality of generated designs

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. We do not transmit your designs or prompts to any third-party servers except:

  • Google Gemini AI: Your prompts are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate OpenSCAD code and images. Please review Google's AI Terms of Service.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. Session data is stored in your browser's local storage for functionality purposes only.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For AI-powered code generation
  • Google Imagen: For image generation

6. Data Retention

Your design history is stored locally until you manually delete it. Cached API responses are automatically cleared after 24 hours.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored designs (via the app interface)
  • Delete your designs at any time
  • Clear the local database by deleting the SQLite file

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • API keys are stored server-side only
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Input validation and sanitization
  • HTTPS encryption (when deployed)

9. Children's Privacy

MakersAI Studio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
